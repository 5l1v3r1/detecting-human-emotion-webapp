About Us

We are working on a Human Emotion and Deception Detection Application. We aim to detect emotion and deception using audio and video.

The main goal of our project was for interviewers to detect when a subject is being deceitful in real-time and with recorded video and audio. One of our main objectives was to create deception detection software which is more portable, accurate, and easy to use than a conventional polygraph machine.
